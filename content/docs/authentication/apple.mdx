## Overview

Allow users to sign in with their Apple ID. Apple Sign In support is coming soon to Clowk.

## Status

Apple Sign In is currently in development. Follow the Clowk changelog for updates.

## What to expect

When available, the Apple integration will:

- Handle the full Sign in with Apple flow
- Support Apple's private relay email (`abc123@privaterelay.appleid.com`)
- Store the user's name from the first sign-in (Apple only sends it once)
- Return a standard Clowk JWT with `"provider": "apple"`

## Current providers

While Apple support is being built, you can use:

- [Google](/docs/authentication/google)
- [GitHub](/docs/authentication/github)
- [Twitter](/docs/authentication/twitter)
- [Email & Password](/docs/authentication/email-password)
